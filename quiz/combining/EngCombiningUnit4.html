so that 表目的或結果

so that為從屬連接詞，引導表示目的或結果的副詞子句。

1. 表示目的，意指「以便於...；為了...」。

  • This explians why waffles in Taiwan are visually stunning and bursting with flavor, so that they appeal to the eye just as much as the taste buds.
    (課文句子)
  • I turned off the TV so that my roommate might study in peace and quiet.
    我關掉了電視，以便我的室友能在平和安靜的狀態下讀書。
    • Tips

    當子句中的so that表目的時，可與in order that互換，但只有in order that能置於句首。

  • Julia got up early so that she could catch the morning flight.
    = Julia got up early in order that she could catch the morning flight.
    = In order that she could catch the morning flight, Julia got up early.
    為了要趕早上的飛機，茱麗亞起得很早。

2. 表示結果，意指「所以...」。

  • Everyone lent a hand, so that the work was finished ahead of schedule.
    每個人都幫了忙，所以工作提前完成了。
  • The new dam controls the waters of the Nile, so that they now run regulary all throught the year.
    新的水壩控制著尼羅河水域，所以河水現在一年到頭都能正常流動。
    • Tips

    也可以so + adj./adv. + that 子句表結果，意指「如此...以致於...」。

  • The story was so interesting that all the students loved it.
    這個故事是如此有趣，以致於所有學生都喜歡它。
    • Tips

    不論表示目的或結果，口語表達時常將so that中的that省略。

  • I'll show you so (that) you can see how it's dont.
    我會示範給你看，讓你看清楚它是如何做成的。表目的
  • Murphy worked very hard, so (that) he became rich in a short time.
    墨菲非常努力工作，所以不久他就致富了。表結果

LET'S PRACTICE Combine sentences with 'so that'.

1.　Put the milk in the refrigerator.
　　We want to make sure it won't spoil.


2.　The little boy pretended to be sick.
　　He wanted to stay home from school.





1. Put the milk in the refrigerator so that it won't spoil.
2. The little boy pretended to be sick so that he might/could stay home from school.
