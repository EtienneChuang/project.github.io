副詞子句-→分詞構句

從屬連接詞引導的子句稱為副詞子句：

連接詞 + S + V　, 　S + V

　副詞子句　　　主要子句

常見的從屬連接詞如表示時間(when/while)、理由(because/as)、條件(though)等

1.為了讓英文句子更簡潔，我們常將「副詞子句」簡化成「分詞構句」。

  • As the Winter X Games gains more fans every year, it had more that 100,000 people come to witness the competitions with their own eyes in 2012.
    (課文例句)

Tips
步驟1 : 刪去副詞子句的主詞(注意:副詞子句與主要子句的主詞必須相同)
步驟2 : 將副詞子句的動詞改為分詞V-ing
步驟3 : 刪除副詞子句的連接詞

→Gaining more fans every year, the Winter X Games had more than 100,000 people come to witness the competitions with their own eyes 2012.
(課文句子)

2.須注意，若原先的動詞是主動語態，改為現在分詞V-ing；如果是被動語態時，則改為過去分詞。

  • When David drove to work yesterday, he had an accident.
    →Driving to work yesterday, David had an accident.
    Driving是主動動作
  • When the book fair was held in Taipei last month, it was a big success. →Held in Taipei last month, the book fair was a big success.
    書展是被舉辦，用held

3.原先的動詞如果是現在完成是或過去完成式，則改寫為having + p.p.。

  • Because Anna had been awake all night, she looked tired this morning.
    →Having been awake all night, Anna looked tired this morning.
    過去完成式had been改寫成Having been

LET'S PRACTICE
Rewrite the following sentences with participial construction.

1. As Rose was feeling sick, she was absent from school yesterday.


2. If you go to bed early every night, you will have more energy everyday.


1. Feeling sick, Rose was absent from school yesterday.

2. Going to bed early every night, you will have more energy every day.
