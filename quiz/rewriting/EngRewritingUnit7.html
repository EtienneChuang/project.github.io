provide, supply, offer 「供應；提供」

provide + 物 + to / for + 人
supply + 物 + to / for + 人

Tips

provide較常與for搭配，supply較常與to搭配
  • Nature's Keepers follows a small conservation team in a park which provides a last refuge for these "dancing bears" of Bulgaris.
    (課文句子)
provide + 人 + with + 物
supply + 人 + with + 物

  • This company provides customers with samples to try their new products.
    = This company provides samples for customers to try their new products.
    這家公司供應樣品給顧客來試用他們的新產品。
  • The dam supplies the citizens with water and power.
    = The dam supplies water and power to the citizens.
    這座水壩供應市民水電。

Tips

supply和provide意義相近，常可互通使用。惟supply較強調大量且長期地供給必需品。另外supply還可當名詞，而provide只能當動詞用。
  • Our medical supplies are running short.
    我們的醫療補給品快要用完了。
offer + 人 + 物
= offer + 物 + to + 人

  • We are now offering you a chance to buy our newest smartphone at half price.
    = We are now offering a chance to you to buy our newest smartphone at half price.
    我們現在提供您一個用半價購買我們最新智慧型手機的機會。

Tips

比較offer中文亦作「提供」，與provide及supply字義相似，但用法上offer表提供者給的未必是接受者所必須的。 _____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

LET'S PRACTICE
Rewrite the following sentences .

1. The hotel supplies free breakfast to every customer. (use 'with' )


2. The organization provides the blind with recording service. (use 'for' )


1. The hotel supplies every customer with free breakfast.

2. The organization provides recording services for the blind.
