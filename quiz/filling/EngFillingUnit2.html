「adj. / adv. / N + 分詞」作複合形容詞

多數的複合形容詞是由兩個以上的字使用連字號「─」連結所構成。複合形容詞可取關係子句，讓文句更簡潔；本次主要介紹「adj. / adv. / N + 分詞」形式的複合形容詞。

　　　　　　　 當動詞原意為「主動」
變化為「現在分詞」		 當動詞原意為「被動」
變化為「過去分詞」
adj. + 分詞 Brendan wants a new hairstyle that looks good.
= Brendan wants a good-looking new hairstyle.
布萊登想要一個好看的新髮型。		 Kelly likes to buy meals that are made ready.
= Kelly likes to buy ready-made meals.
凱莉喜歡買現成的餐點。
adv. + 分詞 Monica is a student who works hard.
= Monica is a hard-working student.
莫妮卡是一位勤奮的學生。 		Of all the service dogs ever trained, the one that was known best was Gretel.
= One of the most well-known service dogs ever trained was Gretel.
(課文句子)
N + 分詞 Cleaning is a job that consumes time.
= Cleaning a a time-consuming job.
清掃是件耗時的工作。		 The Great Wall is the world's largest structure that was made by man.
= The Great Wall is the world's largest man-made structure.
萬里長城是世界上最大的人造建築物。

常見與Well有關的複合形容詞如下:
well-behaved 行為端正的 ; well-dressed 穿著體面的 ;
well-ordered 有秩序的 ; well-rounded 才藝雙全的 ;
well-meant 好意的 ; well-educated 受過良好教育的

LET'S PRACTICE According to the hint fill in the correct compound adjectives.

1. 艾咪每天讀報紙，因此他見多識廣。
Amy is (well, inform) because she reads newspapers every day.

2. 這對夫妻有段長久的婚姻。
The couple has a (long, last) marriage.

2. The couple has a long-lasting marriage.
