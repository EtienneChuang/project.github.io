despite, in spite of, though 「儘管；雖然」

1. 要表達「儘管；雖然」時，常用以下詞彙:

despite　　+ 　N / V-ing
in spite of　 + 　N / V-ing
  • Despite the warning of danger from his father, Marlin, Nemo swims and is caught by a scuba diver
    (課文句子)
  • Despite the cold weather, Sam still went swimming in the lake.
    = In spite of the cold weather, Sam still went swimming in the lake
    儘管天氣很冷，山姆還是去湖中游泳。
  • We found our way home despite losing our map.
    = We found our way home in spite of losing our map.
    儘管弄丟了地圖，我們還是找到回家的路。

Tips
1. despite與in spite of均屬介系詞，後面須接名詞或動名詞。
2. despite, in spite of可置於句首或句中。

2. despite, in spite of後面不能直接接子句，若要接子句必須在子句前加the fact (that)，或者改用從屬連接詞although / though。

despite the fact (that)　　　+　子句
in spite of the fact (that)　 +　子句
although / though　　　　+　子句
  • Despite the fact (that) it rained heavily, we still continued our soccer match.
    = In spite of the fact (that) it rained heavily, we still continued our soccer match.
    = Although / Though it rains heavily, we still continued our soccer match.
    儘管下著大雨，我們仍然繼續進行足球賽。

Tips
1. 以上例句中the fact that皆可省略that。
2. 上述所舉說法如despite the fact (that)等，可置於句首或句中。
