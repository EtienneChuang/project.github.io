VOCABULARY/PHRASE

    瑞士經典 Fondue

    Fondue為法文，意指「融化」，但這個字卻在瑞士發揚光大。在這個以起司聞名的國家，瑞士人習慣以乾燥法保存起司和麵包，冬天時將乾掉的起司添酒加熱成液狀，在沾以乾麵包食用，此即起司鍋(cheese fondue)的起源。瑞士人後來發明另一種風靡全世界的吃法就是巧克力鍋(chocolate fondue)。以下分別介紹兩種fondue的作法與吃法。

    起司鍋 :
    瑞士冬天既漫長又寒冷，這種高熱量的食物對暖身特別有幫助。火鍋的基底是混合格律耶荷(Gruyère)和(Emmentaler)兩種起司和白酒、蒜等原料而成，然後搭配切丁的歐式麵包沾著吃，配料也會出現蔬菜、肉片或香腸。瑞士起司鍋的起司乳脂肪要高於45%以上才可製作，適合搭配的酒有白葡萄酒(white wine)及櫻桃酒(kirsch)。初入口時味道會有些微苦，但濃厚的奶香隨即慢慢從口腔內散開來。

    圖片來源 : 活用空中美語 Feb. 2013 Unit6

    巧克力鍋 :
    巧克力鍋屬於甜點火鍋，瑞士巧克力鍋所使用的苦甜巧克力純度相當高，適合搭配略帶酸味的水果食用。作法為將巧克力碎以隔水加熱的方式慢慢攪拌融化，水溫勿超過60℃，以免造成巧克力油水分離。可適當加入動物性鮮奶油(heavy cream)及甜酒(liqueur)，最後用長柄叉子將水果、餅乾或冰淇淋球沾取巧克力食用。濃郁的巧克力瀰漫著酒香，裹上酸甜的水果嚐起來就像戀愛的滋味。適合與情人一起享用!

Unit 4 : Sweeten Up Your Sweetheart

Valentine's Day is around the corner , which means it's time for you to enjoy a romantic meal with that special someone. A spot of sugar can pave the way for romance, so why not make every meal complete with a tasty treat? Here are two desserts that are guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth.

For brunch or afternoon tea, you can indulge in a Liège waffles. These waffles are made of dough and baked with sugar on the outside to make them sweeter. They also have a rich, chewy texture. You can make waffles more delicious by decorating them with fruits, chocolate, or ice cream. The smell of a fresh waffle is so tempting that you can't resist sharing one with your sweetheart.

It's said that Asians are very visual eaters, so it's no surprise that waffles are becoming popular in Asia. This explains why waffles in Taiwan are visually stunning and bursting with flavor, so that they appeal to the eye just as much as the taste buds. A waffle date is a great way to spend this romantic holiday!

Is there a better finale to your Valentine's Day that a candlelit dinner? You can finish off with a chocolate fondue ─ a pot of melted chocolate. To eat, you put a piece of fruit or pastry onto a stick then dip it in the fondue. You can try dipping cookies or even ice cream into the pot. Whatever you put in comes out hot, sticky , and mouth-watering.

Fondues were invented in Switzerland, where the Swiss originally used melted cheese. They also came up with some fondue etiquette. The rules say it's impolite to "double dip," which means to put something into the pot a second time. You must also be careful not to drop anything into the pot. If the man does it, he must pay for the meal. But if the woman does it, she has to kiss everyone at the table!

Valentine's Day is a perfect time to sweeten up your sweetheart. A Liège waffles or a chocolate fondue will certainly melt in your mouth, and melt your Valentine's heart as well.
