Unit 3 : Florentijn Hofman: Spreading Joy With Art

A giant rubber duck floated into Hong Kong's Victoria Harbor this year,
就在今年，一隻巨大塑膠鴨漂浮在香港的維多利亞港上，

making the busy port a huge bathtub.
使繁忙的港口成為巨大的澡盆。

This scene was not a first, however,
然而，這種場景不是第一次發生，

as the Rubber Duck has been surprising cities since 2007. 因為《橡皮鴨》從西元2007年就為眾多城市帶來驚奇。

Dutch artist Florentijn Hofman is the man behind the Rubber Duck.
荷蘭藝術家佛羅倫汀·霍夫曼正是橡皮鴨背後的推手。

His first commission was to paint a mural on the cooling tower of Kernwasser Wonderland in Germany.
他的第一件委託案是在德國「爐心的奇幻世界」遊樂園的冷卻塔上繪製壁畫。

This 15,000-square-meter painting inspired him to continue to make large art pieces.
這幅面積有一萬五千平方公尺的畫作啟發他持續創作大型藝術作品。

Now his works can be found around the world.
現在世界各地都可以看見他的作品。

He tied 10,000 sandals to Fat Monkey in São Paulo.
他將一萬隻涼鞋綁在一起，創作出位於聖保羅的《胖猴子》。

The 15-meter ape lies outdoors in a park and catches the eye of any passer-by.
這隻15公尺高的猴子躺在戶外的公園中並吸引行人的目光。

The artist also created Big Yellow Rabbit,
這位藝術家也創作了《大黃兔》，

which is made of thousands of roof shingles and is in Örebro, Sweden.
他由上千片屋瓦組成並坐落在瑞典的厄勒布魯。

Unlike many modern artists,
不同於許多現代藝術家，

Hofman chooses everyday subjects that everyone can recognize, especially animals.
霍夫曼選擇人人都能辨識的日常生活主題，特別是動物。

These large cartoon-like creations always get a lot of attention.
這些卡通般的巨大創作總是能吸引許多注意力。

He feels that their cute appearance and giant size can bring people happiness and remind them of their childhood.
他認為作品的可愛外型與巨大尺寸能為人們帶來歡樂並使人憶起童年。

Although Hofman's large and colorful creations have a playful quality,
雖然霍夫曼巨大多彩的作品具有童趣的特質，

the artist has a serious attitude about his work.
藝術家本人對作品的態度卻非常嚴肅。

He says that his art, like the Rubber Duck, is make for the public.
他說他的藝術品，例如《橡皮鴨》，是為了大眾而創作。

Several companies offered to buy the duck,
有幾間公司想買下《橡皮鴨》，

but Hofman rejected them all.
但霍夫曼全數回絕。

When Hofman travels around the world to create huge animal art,
當霍夫曼在世界各地旅行以創造巨大的動物造型藝術品時，

he likes to make it locally.
他喜歡讓作品在地化。

He uses local materials ,
他使用當地的材料，

and enlists enlists local artists and volunteers to help him and his team.
並徵求當地藝術家與志工來協助他和他的團隊。

Once the art is put on public display,
一旦藝術品被公開展示，

people often stop to view it.
人們經常駐足觀賞。

Many say his art makes it easier to start conversations with other onlookers.
許多人說霍夫曼的藝術品讓他們更容易和其他觀賞者開啟對話。

As a result,
因此，

Hofman's art helps strangers to make contact with each other again.
霍夫曼的藝術品幫助陌生人彼此再次產生聯繫。

For Hofman,
對霍夫曼而言，

the audiences' reaction is an essential part of his work.
觀眾的反應是作品不可或缺的一部分。

He invites the public to reconsider things that appear to be a matter of course at first.
他邀請大眾重新思考起出看起來是理所當然的事。

His purpose is simple:
他的目的很簡單 :

to give people a break from their daily lives, and make them smile.
讓人們從日常生活中喘口氣，並讓他們一展笑顏。
