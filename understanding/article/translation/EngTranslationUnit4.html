VOCABULARY/PHRASE

Unit 4 : Sweeten Up Yourr Sweetheart

Valentine's Day is around the corner ,
情人節即將來臨，

which means it's time for you to enjoy a romantic meal with that special someone.
這表示和重要的他/她共享一頓浪漫餐點的時候到了。

A spot of sugar can pave the way for romance,
一點點的糖分能為浪漫做好準備，

so why not make every meal complete with a tasty treat?
所以何不讓美味的好東西完美成就每一餐?

Here are two desserts that are guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth.
這裡提供兩道甜點，保證可以滿足嗜吃甜食的你。

For brunch or afternoon tea,
早午餐或下午茶時光，

you can indulge in a Liège waffles.
你可以享受一下比利時列日鬆餅。

These waffles are made of dough and baked with sugar on the outside to make them sweeter.
這種鬆餅是用麵糰製作，外層裹上糖粉烘焙後使味道更加香甜。

They also have a rich, chewy texture.
而其口感十分濃郁有嚼勁。

You can make waffles more delicious by decorating them with fruits, chocolate, or ice cream.
為了讓鬆餅看起來更美味，你還可以用水果、巧克力或冰淇淋來裝飾。

The smell of a fresh waffle is so tempting that you can't resist sharing one with your sweetheart.
剛出爐的鬆餅香氣如此誘人，你絕對無法抵抗和所愛的人一起分享。

It's said that Asians are very visual eaters,
有人說亞洲人事相當視覺型的饕客，

so it's no surprise that waffles are becoming popular in Asia.
無怪乎鬆餅在亞洲變得如此受歡迎。

This explains why waffles in Taiwan are visually stunning and bursting with flavor,
這也解釋了為何台灣的鬆餅在視覺上總是效果十足、並且口味上充滿變化，

so that they appeal to the eye just as much as the taste buds.
這都是為了使它們吸睛的程度正如同其帶給味蕾的挑動。

A waffle date is a great way to spend this romantic holiday!
來個鬆餅約會是共度這個浪漫節日的的絕佳方式!

Is there a better finale to your Valentine's Day that a candlelit dinner?
在情人節，還有什麼比共享燭光晚餐更適合做為一天的結尾呢?

You can finish off with a chocolate fondue - a pot of melted chocolate.
你可以用一鍋融化的巧克力為這美好的一天作結。

To eat, you put a piece of fruit or pastry onto a stick then dip it in the fondue.
食用時，用竹籤叉著一塊水果或是糕點，然後將其浸在巧克力鍋裡。

You can try dipping cookies or even ice cream into the pot.
你也可以試著用小餅乾或甚至是冰淇淋沾巧克力食用。

Whatever you put in comes out hot, sticky , and mouth-watering.
無論你使用的食材是什麼，最後都會是熱騰騰、黏稠又令人垂涎三尺。

Fondues were invented in Switzerland,
巧克力鍋起源於瑞士，

where the Swiss originally used melted cheese.
最初瑞士人使用融化的起司。

They also came up with some fondue etiquette.
他們也想出一些巧克力鍋的食用禮儀。

The rules say it's impolite to "double dip," which means to put something into the pot a second time.
有條規則是:「沾兩次」─也就是將某項食材二度放進鍋中─是不禮貌的；

You must also be careful not to drop anything into the pot.
而你也要小心別讓其他東西掉進鍋裡，

If the man does it, he must pay for the meal.
如果有男士讓食物掉進鍋裡，那一餐就必須由他付帳；

But if the woman does it, she has to kiss everyone at the table!
若是女士將東西掉入鍋中，那麼她必須親吻同桌的每一個人!

Valentine's Day is a perfect time to sweeten up your sweetheart.
情人節是「甜」滿愛人的心的最佳時機。

A Liège waffles or a chocolate fondue will certainly melt in your mouth,比例時列日鬆餅或巧克力鍋無疑將不只溶於你口，

and melt your Valentine's heart as well.
更能溶化情人的心。
