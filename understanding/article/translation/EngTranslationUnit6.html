Unit 6 : Winter X Games

For fans of winter sports,
對冬季運動迷來說，

nothing can match the excitement of the Winter X Games.
沒什麼可以比「冬季極限運動」來的刺激。

Competitors use skis, snowboards, and snowmobiles to speed down icy slopes and launch into the sky.
參賽選手利用雪屐、雪上滑板與雪上摩托車快速滑下冰上斜坡，且將自己送上天際。

The events include so many gravity-defying tricks that some competitors spend just as much time in the air as they do on the snow.
極限運動比賽包含很多對抗地心引力的特技，而參賽選手在天上與雪地上的時間一樣多。

One exciting event is the Superpipe,
極限運動其中一項刺激的比賽項目為「超級半管」，

which is like a half-pipe,
一座外形像半個水管，

but has much taller walls that allow snowboarders to get higher on their jumps.
但有高聳邊牆的場地，讓雪上滑板選手能跳得更高。

The athletes in the snowmobile event ─ Snocross ─ also spend a good amount of time in the air.
而「雪地障礙賽」的雪上摩托車運動員也花大量時間在空中滯留；

This race involves speeds of over 95 km/h and jumps as long as 40 meters.
這項競速運動包含了95公里時速，以及40公尺的飛越距離。

Just watching the event gets your heart beating fast.
單是觀看這些比賽就足以讓你心跳加速。

The X Games were started as a summer event in 1995 by ESPN, the American sports broadcaster.
極限運動會於西元1995年由美國運動轉播公司ESPN舉辦，

After a huge success,
獲得巨大成功之後，

the winter games were added in 1997.
冬季極限運動於西元1997年加入。

Every year since then, the top winter athletes have come together to put on a great show.
此後，每年都有頂尖的冬季運動員齊聚一堂，聯手完成一場偉大的表演。

We should expect nothing less from the 17th Winter X Games in January 2013.
我們應該可以預料西元2013年1月的第17屆冬季極限運動賽事將精彩可期。

The 2013 Winter X Games will be held from January 24th to 27th in Aspen, Colorado.
2013冬季極限運動會將在1月24日至27於美國科羅拉多州的阿斯彭舉辦。

The games are returning to this famous ski resort community in the mountains for the 12th year
in a row.
這場盛事連續12年在這座位於群山中的滑雪勝地舉行。

Winters here are very cold, with temperature as low as -14°C and tons of snow,
伴隨攝氏負14度低溫，這裡的冬天非常寒冷，

but this is a paradise for winter sports.
不過它是冬季運動的天堂。

More than 200 winter athletes will come with the hopes of winning a gold medal.
將有超過200名冬季運動員，戴著欲贏得金牌的希望來到此地。

Gaining more fans every year,
隨著愛好者數量每年不斷成長，

the Winter X Games had more than 100,000 people come to witness the competitions with their own eyes in 2012.
冬季極限運動在西元2012年有超過十萬的觀眾親臨賽事。

Visitors can ski during the day and attend concerts at night.
造訪者白天可以從事滑雪運動，晚上則能參與演唱會。

Best of all,
最棒的是 :

they get to watch up close the amazing X Games participants who put it all on the line.
他們能夠近距離觀賞卯足全力演出的極限運動選手。

Even if you can't make it to Aspen,
即使你無法親臨阿斯彭，

the games are fantastic for television audiences, as cameras follow every twist and turn of the daring athletes.
但由於攝影機尾隨無畏的運動員，捕捉他們的旋轉和翻滾動作，極限賽事對電視機前的觀眾來說也是精彩萬分。

While these stunts can be extremely dangerous,
這些特技雖然是極度危險，

they're safe to watch from the sofa.
從沙發上觀賞相對很安全。

Pay attention to your local TV schedule so you can catch all the action of the 2013 Winter X Games.
留意你居住所在地的電視時刻表，你便能捕捉2013冬季極限運動完整賽事。
