Unit 5 : Finding NEMO 3D

Long time no "sea"!
「海﹝嗨﹞，好久不見」

Disney brings back one of its classic animated tales, Finding Nemo, in breathtaking 3D.
迪士尼以令人驚嘆的3D模式，重新上映了其中一部經典動畫《海底總動員》。

The film won the 2004 Oscar for Best Animated Feature.
這部片曾在西元2004年贏得奧斯卡最佳動畫片獎。

Now, a whole new audience, as well as old fans, will get a chance to enjoy this heartwarming story with added visual effects from Pixar.
現在，不只以前的影迷，很多新的觀眾也都有機會欣賞這部皮克斯所帶來、溫暖人心兼具視覺效果的動畫故事。

Finding Nemo follows the title character , a clownfish, who wants to show his independence and explore the large ocean world on his own.
《海底總動員﹝尋找尼莫﹞》是一個講述電影同名角色的故事，主角是一隻想要展現獨立，同時也想靠自己的力量探索浩瀚海洋世界的小丑魚。

Despite the warning of danger from his father, Marlin,
儘管爸爸馬林警告他這樣做很危險，

Nemo swims away and is caught by a scuba diver.
尼莫還是一意孤行，而被一位潛水員抓走。

He is kept in a fish tank,
他隨後被放進了一個魚缸中，

where he sees no hope in life.
因而他感到人生毫無希望。

While Marlin is trying every possible way to find his son,
就在此時，馬林試著找尋任何可能的方法要找到他兒子，

Nemo also plans his escape from the tank.
尼莫同時也盤算著如何從魚缸逃離。

Fortunately,
很幸運地，

Marlin bumps into a blue tang fish named Dory,
馬林碰見一隻色刺尾魚多莉

and they form an effective partnership to try to rescue Nemo any way they can.
他們組成了精良、有效的夥伴關係，試著用任何可能的方是拯救尼莫。

Though they encounter many problems in the ocean,
雖然他們在海洋中遭遇許多問題，

they keep on going.
但他們還是繼續前行。

By persevering,
在堅持不懈的努力下，

they finally make it to Nemo and free him again.
他們最後終於成功解救了尼莫，而尼莫也因此再次獲得了自由。

It's not hard to figure out why Finding Nemo is so successful and highly praised.
《海底總動員》為何如此成功並享有高度讚譽不難讓人理解，

Aside from the unforgettable characters and hilarious storyline,
除了有令人難忘的角色及歡樂熱鬧的故事情節外，

the movie illustrates valuable lessons for both children and adults.
這部片也為家長及孩子上了寶貴的一課。

For instance,
例如 :

kids can learn the danger of wandering away from their parents, as Nemo finds trouble when he ignores his father and swim alone.
孩子可以從尼莫身上學到忽視爸爸的要求、獨自離開而遭遇困難的危險；

Parents can learn not to be over protective ,
家長也可以體誤不宜對孩子過度保護，

as Marlin realizes giving Nemo some freedom helped their relationship.
因為馬林終於了解給尼莫一些自由更能增進彼此的關係。

Finally,
最後，

there is a theme for all ages - friendship .
不管你是哪個年齡層，本片的主軸─就是友誼，

Without Dory's help,
若沒有多莉的協助，

Nemo and Marlin couldn't make it.
尼莫和馬林也無法成功。

Their teamwork shows us how cooperation is vital to success and achievement.
他們的協力合作告訴我們 : 團結互助是成功的重要關鍵。

Finding Nemo 3D is not the first classic animation re-released in 3D,
《海底總動員》並不是第一部以3D影像重新上映的經典動畫，

but it might be the most spectacular.
但它很有可能是目前視覺最開闊、場面最壯觀的一部。

The ocean, which is all about depth and space, is a perfect fit for 3D technology.
對於3D技術來說，又深又寬廣的海洋是影像呈現上級完美的素材，

Audiences will wonder at the scenes capturing the ocean's overwhelming size and power.
每一幕海洋所呈現的浩瀚及力量，都將讓觀眾驚訝不已。

So "sea" it like never before in 3D now!
現在，就以從未看過此片的心情來海視3D版吧!
