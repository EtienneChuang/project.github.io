Unit 2 : Dogs Are More Than Man's Best Friend

An assistance dog isn't just a pet:
協助犬並不只是 寵物，

it can be the eyes, ears, friend, and companion of a disabled person.
他們能成為肢障人士的眼睛、耳朵、朋友以及良伴

When most people hear about assistance dogs,
當大多數的人聽到協助犬時

they usually think of guide dogs.
通常會想到導盲犬。

These dogs lead people who are blind,
導盲犬主要負責帶領視障者，

especially in busy or dangerous public places,
尤其在出入繁忙和危險的公共場所時，

and help give them back their independence.
並幫助重新給予他們獨立自主。

Hearing dogs perform a similar function,
導聾犬的職責和導盲犬相似，

but for people who are deaf.
但主要服務對象為聽障者。

Owners of hearing dogs say they feel safer and secure with the help of their trained dog.
導聾犬的主人認為，有了受訓犬的幫忙，他們覺得更加安全及安心。

What's more,
此外，

hearing dogs can alert their owners to phone calls or messages,
導聾犬能夠幫主人留意電話和訊息，

which helps deaf people stay in better tough with friends and loved ones.
這使聽障者和朋友及親近的人保持更加的聯繫。

Last but not least,
最後，

service dogs are the jack of all trades among assistance dogs.
肢障輔助犬則是協助犬中的萬事通。

They can help many kinds of disabled people with a number of particular needs,
他們能幫助達成各種肢障人士的眾多特別需求，

from picking things up off the floor to simply snuggling and making their owner feel loved.

從幫忙撿地上的東西，到只是單純地依偎著主人，讓主人感受到滿滿愛意。

Together service dogs and their owners from a unique bond with each other that no other dogs can match.
在相互陪伴下，肢障輔助犬能和主人間培養出一種其他狗無法比擬的特殊情感連結。

While guide and hearing dogs can perform a new particular tasks related to one disability,
導盲犬和導聾犬能夠執行和某一特病殘障人是有關的特殊工作，

service dogs are even more versatile.
而肢障輔助犬的功能則更多元。

One of the most well-known service dogs ever trained was Gretel.
有史以來最有名的的受訓肢障輔助犬之一非葛蕾特莫屬。

A Labrador Retriever, Gretel became the first service dog in Japan in 1995.
葛蕾特這隻拉不拉多犬在西元1995年成為日本第一隻肢障輔助犬，

Her owner, Mr. Noguchi, was confined to a wheelchair,
牠的主人野口先生被輪椅所困，

so Gretel helped him dress, fetched groceries off the shelf,
因此葛蕾特幫他穿衣、從架上拿取雜貨，

and even opened and closed doors for him.
甚至幫他開關門。

Amazingly,
令人驚訝的是，

Gretel lived to be 17 years old,
葛蕾特活到17歲，

which is a very long life for a dog.
對狗來說是非常長的壽命。

Even when she was at the end of her life,
即使到了生命的尾聲，

she was still on duty at Noguchi's side.
牠仍然在野口先生身邊盡忠職守。

In Taiwan, there is the Taiwan Assistance Dog Association(TADA), which was founded to educate people about assistance dogs and train canines.
在台灣則有專門創建以教育社會大眾並且訓練犬隻的台灣協助犬協會。

Under its training programs,
在他們的訓練計畫下，

puppies are taught to be assistance dogs for disabled people.
幼犬被訓練以成為殘障人士的協助犬。

Though its programs only started two years ago,
雖然訓練計畫僅開始兩年，

TADA has already attracted many people's interest in assistance dogs.
台灣協助犬協會已經吸引了許多人對協助犬的興趣與關心。

With the public's emotional and financial support,
有了社會大眾精神及經濟上的支持贊助，

TADA hopes that soon there will be enough assistance dogs trained to care for the disabled in Taiwan.
台灣協助犬協會希望很快地能有足夠的結訓協助犬，幫助台灣有需要的殘障人士。
