VOCABULARY/PHRASE

    地球像是一個充滿天然寶藏的大寶庫。然而，這些自然的奇蹟正面臨威脅並迫切需要保護。Discovery HD頻道的Nature's Keepers帶我們跨越五大洲見識壯觀的景色，並認是充滿熱忱的人們奉獻其一生於生態環境的保育。

  • Texas
    德州充滿了神祕美國西部風情，它的面積比法國還大，至今仍然保留了大片的原始野地。在奇瓦瓦沙漠(Chihuahuan Desert)的生物學家陶德專門研究人見人怕的德州響尾蛇，並希望導正人們觀念，停止迫害這種對生態系有重要功能的動物。
  • Mexico
    墨西哥擁有各式各樣的地形:有海岸平原和高山；有沙漠，也有雨林，因此這裡的生態系變化多端。在潘塔拉古納(Punta Laguna)，一個馬雅社區負責追蹤蜘蛛猴的生態；在拉曼查(La Mancha)保育區，一名科學家紀錄鳥類的歌聲和鳴叫聲，想藉此更加了解鳥類的行為。
  • Sri Lanka
    斯里蘭卡位於印度西南方50公里的印度洋，有「絢爛耀眼島」(the resplendent island)的稱號。但由於缺乏有遠見的自然資源，島上的生物多樣性正遭逢破壞。有一群奉獻付出的大自然熱愛者，下定決心要保護它。
  • Abu Dhabi
    阿布達比北以波斯灣為邊界，被沙烏地阿拉伯和阿曼王國環繞，擁有豐富的自然資源，包括碧綠的海水，崎嶇的哈菲特山(Jebel Hafeet)以及壯麗的沙漠。然而不到半世界期間，由於油礦的開採，阿布達比經歷了快速的經濟與都市發展。Abu Dhabi原意為「瞪羚之父」，且看保護區巡邏員和生物學家如何在直升機的協助下追捕瞪羚，並將這象徵阿拉伯文化的動物野放回到自然環境中。
  • Iceland
    冰島擁有被冰火打造而成的壯麗地形，它遠在北大西洋上，雖然被劃入歐洲範圍，但是幾乎已接觸到北極圈。在這個緯度下，冰島受制於不斷變化的氣候，和仍然活躍的火山底土所影響，擁有非常獨特的地貌。冰島人口約30萬人，對於環保議題心態複雜，時而模糊。他們很自豪於祖先所征服的獨特環境，面對變化莫測的大自然態度相當謙虛和敬畏；然而有時會為了經濟利益而忽視生態。這片原始環境的保育，端視人們如何巧妙維持生態和經濟的平衡。而在冰島各地奉獻心力的科學家，正拾起這個挑戰

Unit 7 : Nature's Keepers

圖片來源:活用空中美語
Jan. 2013 Unit12

Bulgaria, in Southeastern Europe, is home to many nationally loved animals like bears, storks, and vultures. However, these animals have been mistreated over the years. Fortunately, there are many efforts to preserve these species. Discovery's Nature's Keepers lets us explore how conservationists are protecting nature in Bulgaria.

The brown bear is one of Europe's largest predators. In the past, the animal was used for entertainment at the circus. This no longer occurs, but the bears have lost a lot of their natural instincts and confidence. Nature's keepers follows a small conservation team in a park which provides a last refuge for these "dancing bears" of Bulgaria. The team helps the bears restore their body clock by encouraging hibernation. They also simulate wild conditions by placing food in wooden tunnel, forcing the bears to obtain it on their own. This helps the bears learn how to live in the wild. Bulgaria adores its bears, to say the least, but also has concerns about other species.

A wild bird refuge center in the south of Bulgaria receive over 1,200 birds each year. Some 90 percent of them are brought to the center after being found near the road. One of the main birds at the center is the stork, which represents good luck to the people of Bulgaria. However, storks are sometimes the victims of high-voltage electrical wires and arrive at the center as patients. Luckily, there are many caring and responsible volunteers who use all their energy and skills to save the birds.

圖片來源:活用空中美語Jan. 2013 Unit12

The animal lovers in Bulgaria also pay attention to rescuing the endangered vulture. Many hunters used to see the bird as a pest, as it ate dead animals, but it actually helps clean up the landscape and prevents the spread of infectious diseases. So, now the nation is keeping this special bird safe from harm and helping it breed at a stable rate. Bulgaria's great effort to protect its beloved animals gives them a great chance to live on.
