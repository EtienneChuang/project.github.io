classic [ˋklæsɪk] adj. 經典的

The Beatles made many classic songs in their career, such as "Let it be".


as well as 以及 ; 和

A pet dog will make you happy as well as protect your home.
一隻寵物狗將會讓你感到開心和保護你的家。


title character [ˋkærɪktɚ] n. (電影、書籍等)同名角色

Charlie Bucket is the title character of Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.


despite, in spite of, though 儘管 ; 雖然


warning [ˋwɔrnɪŋ] n. 警告

Nowadays there are many health warnings on cigarette boxes.


bump into [bʌmp] phr. 無意中遇到

It was a nice surprise for Daivd when he bumped into his favorite idol at the mall.


effective [ɪˋfɛktɪv] adj. 有效的

The effective sales team makes a lot of money for their company.


partnership [ˋpɑrtnɚ͵ʃɪp] n. 合作關係 ; 合夥關係

Venus and Serena Williams have a good partnership when they play doubles tennis.


illustrate [ˋɪləstret] v. 舉例說明 ; 闡明
(illustrate-illustrated-illustrated)

Teachers illustrate the meaning of vocabulary by giving clear examples.


valuable [ˋvæljʊəb!] adj. 寶貴的 ; 有價值的

David is a valuable member of the team because he always has good ideas.


For instance 例如

My mom knows how to cook many desserts, for instance, pumpkin pie.
我媽知道如何製作例如南瓜派等許多點心


protective [prəˋtɛktɪv] adj. 保護的

Masks are protective against air pollution while you are riding a scooter.


friendship [ˋfrɛndʃɪp] n. 友誼

Gina liked her new coworkers and hoped to form friendships with them.


cooperation [ko͵ɑpəˋreʃən] n. 合作 ; 協力

The cooperation and effort of the team help them get through challenges.


vital [ˋvaɪt!] adj. 極其重要的 ; 不可缺少的

Getting enough sleep is vital for a healthy body.
